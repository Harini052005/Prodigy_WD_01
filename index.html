FAQ

How can I book a flight on your website?

Booking is very easy! Simply visit our homepage and enter your departure and destination cities, along with your travel dates, Click on the "search" button, and you will see a list of available flights. Choose the one that suitable for you best and follow the simple steps to complete your booking.

What should I do if I need to make changes to my hotel reservation?

Making changes to your hotel reservtion is hassel-free. Log in to your account, go to the "My Bookings" section, and select the reservation you want to modify. From there, you can usally find options to edit dates, room preferences, or even cancel the reservation if you needed.

Are there any travel restricctions or COVID-19 guidelines I should be aware of for my destination?

Stay informed about travel restrictions and COVID-19 guidelines by checking our dedicated travel advisory page. We provide up to date information on entry requirements, quarantine ruels, and health protocols for various destinations. It's essentials to review this information before planning your trip.

What payment methods are accepted for bookings on your site?

We accept variety of payment methods, including major credit cards, debit cards, and popular digital wallets. During the booking process you can choose your preferred payment option. Rest assured, our payment gateway is secure and ensures the safety of your financial information.
